Probability Calculation

To calculate the probability of student having blue eyes, divide the number of students with blue eyes by the total number of students. Similarly, we can calculate the probability that student in his/her third year, and the probability of male student.

The probability that a student has blue eyes given he is a man can be calculated by dividing the number of male students with blue eyes by the number of male students. This is a conditional probability. The probability that a student has blue eyes given she is a woman can be calculated similarly.
